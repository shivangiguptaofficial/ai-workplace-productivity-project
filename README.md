
# AI-Driven Workplace Productivity & Process Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview
This project simulates an internal AI adoption initiative within a business environment. As part of this initiative, I tested and deployed ChatGPT across core operational workflows—focusing on conversational data analysis, automated documentation, internal change management, and executive reporting.

---

## 🛠️ Project Structure & Workflow

### 1. AI Workspace Setup & Custom Instructions
* **Tool:** ChatGPT (Custom Projects feature)
* **Framework:** Configured custom instructions using the **"5W & How"** framework to establish a consistent, professional, and goal-oriented persona for internal business communications and data tasks.

### 2. Employee Performance Data Analysis
* **Dataset:** `employee_performance.xlsx` (containing employee metrics across departments: Projects Completed, Average Rating, Total Sales, and Engagement Scores).
* **Key Tasks:** Uploaded and queried the dataset using conversational prompt engineering without manual sorting or complex Excel formulas.
* **Key Findings:** 
  * Successfully identified top-performing employees based on engagement scores and project output.
  * Calculated aggregate financial metrics (such as total sales generated by top-engaged employees).

### 3. Business Documentation & Internal Communications
* **Internal Memo:** Authored a 200-word memo titled *"Recognizing Our Most Engaged Team Members"*, highlighting key achievements and incorporating visual AI elements.
* **Announcement Email:** Drafted a professional company-wide email to roll out the internal report with an inclusive and motivating tone.
* **Executive Summary Report:** Compiled a comprehensive summary report outlining the complete workflow, automation benefits, and efficiency gains of integrating AI into day-to-day business operations.

---

## 📊 Key Results & Business Value
* **Efficiency:** Demonstrated a significant reduction in time spent drafting routine internal reports and memos.
* **Data Accessibility:** Proved that conversational AI can quickly extract actionable business insights from unstructured or raw tabular datasets.
* **Change Management:** Established a scalable template for introducing new technological tools through clear stakeholder communication.
